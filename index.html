About

Welcome to the 16th EECS Research Students Symposium.

EECS-2025 is an initiative of the Division of EECS and is primarily a forum for presentations by senior research students (expecting to graduate in 2025) involving the Departments of Computational and Data Sciences (CDS), Computer Science and Automation (CSA), Electrical Communication Engineering (ECE), Electrical Engineering (EE), Electronic Systems Engineering (ESE) and the Robert Bosch Centre for Cyber-Physical Systems (RBCCPS).

We invite researchers, faculty members, industry professionals, and research students working in EECS to participate and provide their feedback. We especially invite the alumni of the division to join us for this event.
